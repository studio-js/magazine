About Habitus

Taste is not only choice. It is an embodied order.

Habitus is a digital magazine that reads the conditions that form taste through art, technology, design, beauty, and philosophy. It looks past quick recommendations toward the origins of lasting sense, the social arrangements behind liking, and the language of repeated choices.

01

Issues Are Scenes of Taste

The latest edition is No. 03 · Quiet Instruments. Each issue follows one condition that forms taste instead of simply grouping categories.

02

Lists Are Ways of Distinction

Dates, titles, space, and repeated rules decide what stays close and what moves away. The archive is an edited surface where the order of taste appears.

03

Motion Must Carry Meaning

Geometry and motion are used when they show how sense is assembled, not as decoration. A screen should last through precise intervals rather than performance.
